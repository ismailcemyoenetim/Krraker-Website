Kraker

Kraker is a design practice with its own pace and priorities.

Kraker creates graphic work, a few t-shirts, and a few objects—for now.

It knows exactly what it doesn't want to do.

It cares about cultures, subcultures, people, and their ways of being.

It makes what it believes in—and believes in what it makes.

Kraker tries to fail—nothing less.
